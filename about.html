Sara Harvey is a former newspaper reporter, barista and vitamin factory security guard, now writing and drawing comics in Rochester, New York (by way of Brooklyn). Her work has appeared in The Rumpus, Hobart Pulp, Spiralbound, The Belladonna, The Junction, Centifictionist, Parallel History Museum and more.

Her skills were forged in the warm fires of the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater, Center for Cartoon Studies, SVA continuing ed and Flatiron School (coding/front end web dev).
