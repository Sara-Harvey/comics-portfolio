Sara Harvey is a former newspaper reporter, barista and vitamin factory security guard, now writing and drawing comics in Rochester, New York (by way of Brooklyn). Her work has appeared in The Rumpus, Hobart, Spiralbound, The Belladonna, Centifictionist, Parallel History Museum and more.

Her skills were forged in the warm fires of the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater, Center for Cartoon Studies, and SVA continuing ed.

Her army of orcs, however, was forged in the deep fires of Isengard.
